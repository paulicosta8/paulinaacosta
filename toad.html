Toad's Survival Guide

A travel guide, poster series, postcard set, and trifold brochure for the Mushroom World Travel Agency, a fictional travel brand built for a game development class.

Role

Brochure designer · Copywriter · 6-person team

Context

Game Development Class · 2025

Deliverables

Travel posters · Postcards · Brochure · Booklet

Tools

Adobe InDesign · Figma

The project

A travel agency for the Mushroom World.

The Mushroom World Travel Agency needed a complete brand kit to market five Nintendo game-world destinations to would-be tourists. Each location got its own poster, postcard, and booklet entry written from Toad's firsthand perspective, with star reviews, field notes, and local creature warnings included.

Travel posters

Five destinations, five moods.

Each location got a cinematic travel poster. All of the art was created by one of my teammates on Procreate, and we then overlaid it with bold display type. The Mushroom World Travel Agency badge appears in the corner of each.

Postcards

Greetings from the Mushroom World.

A matching postcard for each destination, using the classic "Greetings from…" large-letter style with location photography masked inside the letterforms.

Trifold brochure

The official visitor's guide.

I designed the trifold brochure, which showcases all five destinations with highlights, descriptions, and the travel agency's branding. The goal was for it to feel like something you'd actually grab at a tourism kiosk.

Survival guide booklet

A guidebook written by Toad himself.

The survival guide is the heart of the project: a full travel booklet written from Toad's perspective, complete with star reviews, field notes about local creatures, and "Two Cents" quotes from Nintendo characters. I contributed the copywriting for several entries.
