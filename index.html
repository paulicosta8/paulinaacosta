About

I'm a multidisciplinary designer based in NJ/NY.

I recently graduated from Northeastern University with a B.S. in Computer Science and Design, with a concentration in Interaction Design. I fell in love with designing cool things while keeping users in mind. Throughout my past experiences, which included co-ops and leadership positions, I've designed brand systems, event identities, merchandise, packaging, social content, and digital products, often multitasking but never cutting corners on quality.

Outside of work, I'm passionate about my Filipino culture. I served on the e-board of Barkada, Northeastern's Filipino student organization, for two years and as a mentor for four years. I started as their Media Specialist in 2023, connecting my love for design and my culture through graphics and merchandise. As Vice President the next year, I continued designing events to bring my community together. That passion for design and my culture came full circle this past semester when I developed my senior capstone project, ISA, an e-commerce site spotlighting Filipino-American fashion brands.

Through these experiences, I realized how design can be used to connect, support, and build up people and their communities. That is exactly why I want to continue working in design.

Beyond that, I love travelling, fashion, eating my way through any city, and collecting trinkets and blind boxes!
