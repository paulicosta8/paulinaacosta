About

I'm a multidisciplinary designer based in NJ/NY.

I recently graduated from Northeastern University with a B.S. in Computer Science and Design, with a concentration in Interaction Design. I fell in love with designing cool things while keeping users in mind. Throughout my past experiences, I've designed brand systems, event identities, merchandise, packaging, social content, and digital products, often multitasking but never cutting corners on quality.

Outside of work, I'm passionate about my Filipino culture. I served on the e-board of Barkada, Northeastern's Filipino student organization, for two years and as a mentor for four years. I started as their Media Specialist in 2023, connecting my love for design and my culture through graphics and merchandise. That intersection continued this past semester when I developed my senior capstone project, ISA, an e-commerce site spotlighting Filipino-American fashion brands. Through it, I realized how design can be used to connect and support people and their communities.

Also, I love travelling, collecting trinkets and blind boxes, and fashion!
