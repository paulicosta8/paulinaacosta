Intergalactic Retirement Home

A social deduction tabletop RPG set in a deep-space retirement home, where retired cosmic legends with memory loss investigate a murder. I designed the cards, character sheets, encyclopedia booklet, and presentation.

Role

Card design · Booklet · Presentation

Team

6-person team

Players

7 (6 residents + 1 overseer)

Structure

8 rounds across 2 acts

Overview

Retired cosmic legends. Memory loss. One dead roommate.

Players take on the role of retired cosmic legends: a dementia-ridden war horse general, a 7,000-year-old tortoise archaeologist, an ex-mafia birdman, a forgetful alien genius, all stuck together in a drifting deep-space retirement facility.

The mystery kicks off when their leader is found dead. Players use Identity Shift Cards, Memory Fragments, and Event Cards to investigate, deceive, and build a collective narrative where not even the players know what's true.

Card system

Three card types, three mechanics.

Each card type has its own color and purpose. Identity Shift Cards (pink) alter a player's behavior mid-game. Memory Fragments (green) are clues that may be true, false, or delusional. Event Cards (red) introduce randomized disruptions that shift the whole table's reality.

Character cards

Seven residents, seven secrets.

Each character has a public-facing card with their overview and a hidden trait only they know. That asymmetry is what drives the social deduction throughout the game.

Encyclopedia of the Retirement Home

The in-universe lore book.

A companion booklet covering the retirement home's mysterious origins and each species' history. The blue hardcover and cream interior make it feel like a real reference book you might find drifting through space.

Pitch presentation

A pitch deck that matched the game's world.

The full pitch deck was designed to match the game's tone: clean, editorial typography with a sci-fi edge. Each section was laid out to communicate the game's world just as much as its rules.

Instruction manual

Everything a new resident needs to know

A single-page reference sheet covering the full game: the premise, all six characters, the memory system, identity shifts, voting, event cards, and materials list. Designed to be handed to players before the game starts.
